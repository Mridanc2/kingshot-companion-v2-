💡 Tips
⚖️Mix your joiner heroes: 2 Lethality (Chenko/Yeonwoo) + 2 Attack (Amane). Same buffs add, Attack × Lethality multiply — never stack just one type.
🎲Skip chance-based heroes as a joiner (Zoe, Petra, Marlin) — only one ever triggers, wasting a slot.
🚩Join order matters — the rally locks the first 4 joiner skills as people join, so fill both buff types early. Check the rally's yellow flag first.
📚Max your lead hero's first Expedition skill — only that one skill counts when you join.
🏇Diana speeds your march — for smaller / further-out accounts she cuts the return trip from the Bear, getting you back into rallies ~25% faster, so more total damage over the event.
🏹Archers are your main DPS — go mostly archers (5/10/80), and always T10 before T9 (~40% more damage).
🏰Pre-activate Attack/Lethality buffs (and rally capacity if leading) before the event.
🛡️Plan with the Alliance tab — build a Wave to maximize how many rallies your alliance can run at once.
